Mac and cheese

Home

Description

This classic mac and cheese recipe is creamy, cheesy, and comforting. Perfect for a quick weeknight dinner or a cozy weekend meal.

Ingredients

  • 200g elbow macaroni
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 cups milk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp paprika (optional)
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F). Cook macaroni according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large saucepan, melt butter over medium heat. Stir in flour and cook for 1-2 minutes until bubbly.
  3. Gradually whisk in milk, cooking until the mixture thickens, about 5-7 minutes.
  4. Remove from heat and stir in cheddar cheese, Parmesan cheese, salt, pepper, and paprika until melted and smooth.
  5. Add cooked macaroni to the cheese sauce and stir to combine.
  6. If using breadcrumbs, transfer mac and cheese to a baking dish, sprinkle breadcrumbs on top, and bake for 20-25 minutes until golden and bubbly. If not using breadcrumbs, simply serve the mac and cheese immediately.
