Bang Bang chicken sliders

Home

Description

These Bang Bang Chicken Sliders are a delightful fusion of crispy chicken, creamy sauce, and fresh toppings, all nestled in soft slider buns. Perfect for parties, game days, or any casual gathering, these sliders are sure to be a hit!

Ingredients
  • 2 chicken breasts, cut into small pieces
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • Oil for frying
  • 8 slider buns
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Sliced cucumbers
  • Sliced red onions
  • For the Bang Bang Sauce:
    • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
    • 2 tbsp sweet chili sauce
    • 1 tbsp sriracha sauce (adjust to taste)
    • 1 tsp honey
    • 1 tsp lime juice

Instructions

  1. Marinate the chicken pieces in buttermilk for at least 30 minutes.
  2. In a bowl, mix flour, paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Heat oil in a deep pan for frying.
  4. Remove chicken from buttermilk, dredge in the flour mixture, and fry until golden brown and cooked through. Drain on paper towels.
  5. In a small bowl, mix all the ingredients for the Bang Bang sauce.
  6. To assemble the sliders, spread some Bang Bang sauce on the bottom half of each bun. Add a piece of fried chicken, lettuce, cucumber slices, and red onion. Top with the other half of the bun.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy!
